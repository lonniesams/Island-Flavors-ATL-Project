About Us

Island Flavors ATL is an online-only Jamaican restaurant delivering authentic Jamaican cuisine across the Atlanta metro area. Our dishes are inspired by the vibrant culinary traditions of Kingston, Jamaica, and crafted by chefs who have been influenced by some of the top Caribbean culinary masters. We bring the island vibes and rich flavors to your home with every order.

Our mission is to deliver bold, flavorful dishes that transport you to the heart of the Caribbean with every bite. From Jerk Chicken to Ackee and Saltfish, every dish is made with passion, authenticity, and a dedication to preserving the true essence of Jamaican cuisine. We’re proud to share the culture and warmth of Jamaica with the Atlanta community, one delicious meal at a time.
